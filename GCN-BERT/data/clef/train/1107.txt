The sealed tube was heated to 50Â° C. for 14 days and the precipitation formed. After cooling, the precipitate was collected by filtration and washed with cold 1:1 acetonitrile/water to give 2,5-dimethyl-N-(4-(2-oxo-1,2,3,4-tetrahydroquinolin-6-yl)thiazol-2-yl)benzamide (0.096 g, 62%) as a beige solid.
